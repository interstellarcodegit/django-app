Background Info

Mang'u High School is a Kenyan Roman Catholic National High School
established in 1925, located in Kiambu County along the Nairobi-Thika Highway six kilometres from Thika, Kenya.
Mang'u High School is ranked among the top schools nationwide in Kenya Certificate of
Secondary Education[1] and has many eminent alumni including one Kenyan president,
several vice presidents,
Central Bank of Kenya governor and a former Cardinal.
